# Remote & hybrid work — Internal FAQ (2026)

**Who is eligible?**  
Teams that can meet sprint commitments with ≤2 in-office days per month may apply. Manager + HR approval.

**Hardware stipend**  
$1200 / employee / rolling 3-year window for Apple-silicon devices only. Reimbursement via the internal
“GearDesk” portal; receipts required within 14 days.

**Confidentiality**  
Customer data, model evaluation runs, and unreleased product names (including codenames) must never
appear in personal cloud notes or non-approved channels. When in doubt, use the on-prem wiki only.

**Return-to-site notice**  
Leadership can require 3 consecutive on-site days for design reviews, with 10 business days of notice
except in security incidents (immediate recall).
